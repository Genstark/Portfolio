Project Overview

I developed an advanced object detection system with a range of 2 kilometers to enhance safety and operational efficiency for Indian Railways. This system uses sophisticated algorithms and sensors to identify and track potential hazards along the railway tracks, such as vehicles and obstacles. By providing real-time alerts to locomotive pilots, especially in adverse weather conditions, the system improves decision-making and situational awareness. This technology not only helps prevent accidents but also promotes smoother operations and reduces delays, significantly enhancing the overall safety of the railway network.
