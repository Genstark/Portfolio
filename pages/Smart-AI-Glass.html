Overview

Project Overview

Smart AI Glass is an assistive technology project designed to empower visually impaired individuals. The system uses computer vision to detect and identify objects in the wearer's environment, then communicates findings through real-time audio feedback. The project was published in IEEE Xplore, validating its research contribution.
