Project Overview

I developed a smart glasses program that uses real-time audio cues to help visually impaired individuals identify nearby objects and navigate more confidently. The system employs advanced object recognition to detect and describe objects like doorways, stairs, and vehicles, providing clear audio feedback on the object's type, distance, and direction. This technology is designed for various settings, both indoors and outdoors, adapting seamlessly to different environments and lighting conditions. By offering essential spatial awareness, the program enhances users' independence and sense of security, allowing them to navigate daily activities with greater freedom.
